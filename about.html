About Us

Our Mission

Project CTRL was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to empower individuals to take control of their lives through accessible tools and community support.

Our Values

"We don't always have time to chase 'innovation.' Sometimes, we're just trying to keep our heads above water. I made a tool to make that part easier."

— Genavive Ramirez, Founder

Project CTRL is built for real people facing real challenges: business owners balancing it all, individuals supporting their families, newcomers learning the ropes, and innovators with ideas but limited resources. Take back control of your life, your work, and your future, especially when systems fail you.
