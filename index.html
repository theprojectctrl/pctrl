Our Story

Founded by Genavive Ramirez, Project CTRL emerged from a personal mission to streamline success for small businesses and innovators. After helping her mother's business and launching two successful initiatives (STEM by Step and Hearts of Tomorrow), she developed a formula to replace guesswork with guided pathways — giving entrepreneurs and creators their time back while maximizing impact.
